---

## 1. Welcome and opening

_Chair's welcome; any introductions; confirmation the meeting is quorate._

## 2. Apologies for absence

_Record apologies received and accepted._

## 3. Declarations of interest

_Trustees declare any interests relevant to today's agenda. Record the
interest, the trustee, and how it was handled (e.g. withdrew from the
discussion / did not vote)._

## 4. Minutes of the previous meeting

_Confirm the minutes of the meeting held on [date] as a correct record.
Note who proposed and seconded their approval._

## 5. Matters arising and action log

_Review the action log from previous meetings: each action, owner, due date,
and current status. Carry forward anything outstanding._

## 6. Safeguarding _(standing item — every meeting)_

_Recent updates, concerns or incidents (anonymised here — keep detail in the
board's confidential record), and the board's assurance that safeguarding
responsibilities are being met._

## 7. Safety _(standing item — every meeting)_

_Safety updates and priorities, premises/activity safety, and any incidents
or near-misses and the actions taken._

## 8. Lead Volunteer / leadership team report

_Headlines from the section/leadership teams: membership, programme,
volunteers, anything needing the board's attention._

## 9. Finance — Treasurer's report

_Budget vs actual, current balances and reserves, significant income and
expenditure, any decisions the board is asked to make, and progress towards
the annual accounts._

## 10. Risk register review

_Is the risk register up to date? Review high-severity risks, any change
since the last meeting, and any new risks needing the board's attention._

## 11. Compliance

_Volunteer compliance with mandatory learning and disclosure (DBS / PVG)
requirements; data protection (GDPR); insurance; any regulatory returns
(e.g. to the Charity Commission)._

## 12. Property and premises

_Where applicable: the state of the HQ / hut / equipment, leases, insurance,
maintenance and any capital projects._

## 13. Group business _(GROUP boards — delete if not a Group)_

- **Section health:** numbers and waiting lists across Squirrels, Beavers,
  Cubs, Scouts (and any Explorer Unit / Network), leader vacancies.
- **Subscriptions and fees:** subs levels, gift-aid, hardship support.
- **Fundraising:** activities, grants, targets.
- **Volunteer recruitment:** vacancies and recruitment plans.
- **AGM preparation:** date, papers, trustee elections (as the AGM approaches).

## 13. District business _(DISTRICT boards — delete if not a District)_

- **Group health:** Group openings, closures and support to struggling Groups.
- **District team and appointments:** adult appointments, awards, vacancies.
- **District events and activities:** camps, competitions, training.
- **District-held assets and property:** equipment stores, campsites, finances.
- **Growth and development:** the District development plan and progress.

## 13. County / Area / Region business _(delete if not a County/Area/Region)_

- **Oversight of Districts:** District health, support and escalations.
- **Strategy and development plan:** progress against the County/Area/Region plan.
- **Property and major assets:** campsites, activity centres, larger reserves
  and investments.
- **Employed staff / HR:** where the charity employs staff (if any).
- **Events, awards and appointments** at County/Area/Region level.
- **Relationship with Headquarters** and the wider movement.

<!-- ===================================================================== -->

## 14. Sub-committees and working groups

_Reports from any sub-committees or working groups, and any decisions the
board is asked to ratify._

## 15. Any other business (AOB)

_Items raised that were not on the agenda._

## 16. Date and venue of the next meeting

_e.g. Wednesday 11 June 2026, 19:30, Scout HQ._

## 17. Close

_Time the meeting closed._
