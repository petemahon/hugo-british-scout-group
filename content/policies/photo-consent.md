## Why we ask

Camps, badge presentations and parades are naturally photogenic. Sharing
photos helps families see what their child has been up to and shows
prospective members what life in the Group is like. We also owe a duty
of care to the young people in those photos.

## What we ask of parents and carers

When a young person joins, you are asked to set a photo-consent
preference on their record. You can change it at any time: contact the
Group Lead Volunteer and we will update the record and remove any
already-published photos that include your child.

## How we use photos

- **Our website.** Galleries are reviewed before publication. Captions
  never identify a young person by name.
- **Group communications.** Newsletters and Group-wide messages use the
  same consent setting.
- **Scouts UK and District.** Where District or Headquarters ask for
  photos, we only forward images of young people whose families have
  given consent.

## What we don't do

- We don't sell photos.
- We don't embed third-party tracking on this site.

## Right to be forgotten

Email the Group Lead Volunteer and we will remove any photo at your
request, no questions asked.
