## How we keep in touch

The Group's official channels are this website and the messaging route
your section leaders have told you about. Notices that affect your child
will always reach you through an official channel - not only through
social media.

## Our use of social platforms

Edit this section to reflect what your Group actually does. A common
position for a Group that values privacy:

- We keep a presence only on platforms the Group controls.
- We post photos of young people only in line with our
  [photo-consent policy](../photo-consent/).
- We never share a young person's full name, school or contact details.
- We don't embed third-party tracking or social widgets on this site.

## Leaders, young people and online contact

Leaders follow the Yellow Card rules on online contact: communication
about Scouting goes through parents and official Group channels, not
private one-to-one messaging with young people. See our
[safeguarding statement](../safeguarding/).
