## What data we hold

We hold the minimum data needed to run Scouts: young people's names and
dates of birth, parent and carer contact details, and dietary and
medical needs. We do not collect anything we do not need.

## Where it lives

Membership data is held in Online Scout Manager (OSM) under The Scout
Association's data-protection terms. We do not run our own database and
this website has no backend - it cannot collect or store your data.

## Who we share it with

We share data only with the section leaders who need it and, where
required, with District or Headquarters for permits and events. We never
sell or pass data to third parties for marketing.

## Your rights

You can ask to see, correct or delete the data we hold about your family.
Email the Group Lead Volunteer and we will action it - usually within 30
days, in line with UK GDPR.
