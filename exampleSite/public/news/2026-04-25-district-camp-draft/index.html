District Camp 2026 — heads up

25 April 2026 by Skip
  • Scouts
  • Explorers

District Camp this year is at Gilwell-on-Sea over the late May bank holiday weekend. Booking opens on 1 May for our Group; expect the usual scrum.

Activities provisionally include sailing, climbing, archery, and the night hike (which the Explorers have been agitating for since last year). Kit list and full itinerary will follow once District confirm catering arrangements.
