Cubs — Summer Term 2026

Cubs · 15 April 2026 – 15 July 2026 · British School in Anytown Summer Term

District approved

This term we’re building towards the Naturalist activity badge and a weekend Pack camp at Anytown Common. A typical Cubs term: fire-lighting, map work, a community visit, and plenty of outdoor time. Half-term falls in week 8 — no meeting that week.

Wk Date Meeting Notes / kit
1 Wed 15 Apr
Welcome back & investiture
  • Skills for life
  • Beliefs and attitudes
 Bring your necker. New Cubs welcome — uniform optional for week 1.
2 Wed 22 Apr
Map reading & local geography
  • Outdoor and adventure
  • Skills for life
 Bring a pencil. We'll be working in pairs on Ordnance Survey extracts of the local hill.
3 Wed 29 Apr
Fire-lighting safety
  • Outdoor and adventure
  • Try new things
 Long sleeves and closed shoes. Activity at the hut — no off-site travel.
4 Wed 6 May
Community link visit Cancelled
  • Community impact

Visit postponed to the autumn term; meeting cancelled.

 Visitor from a local community group — short talk, then Q&A.
5 Wed 13 May
Knot relay & team challenge
  • Teamwork and leadership
  • Skills for life
 Outdoor if dry; we'll move into the hut if it's wet.
6 Wed 20 May
Naturalist activity badge — part 1
  • Outdoor and adventure
  • Live up to our values
 Local walk. Sturdy shoes and a water bottle.
7 Wed 3 Jun
Naturalist activity badge — part 2
  • Outdoor and adventure
  • Live up to our values
 Habitat survey at the hut grounds. Bring a notebook.
8 Wed 17 Jun
Cooking on a single hob
  • Skills for life
  • Have adventures
 Camp preparation. We'll cook the sausages-and-pasta meal we'll be using on camp.
9 Wed 24 Jun
Pioneering basics
  • Teamwork and leadership
  • Outdoor and adventure
 Lashings and frapping turns. Outdoor — please bring layers.
10 Sat 4 Jul
Pack camp — Anytown Common
  • Have adventures
  • Outdoor and adventure
  • Teamwork and leadership
 Saturday-Sunday camp. See the kit list link below for the full pack list. Leaders' phone numbers will be circulated separately.
11 Wed 15 Jul
End-of-term party & badge presentations
  • Beliefs and attitudes
  • Live up to our values
 Casual dress. Final meeting of the term.
Week 8 is half-term (no meeting). Pack camp is in week 10 — kit list link below.
