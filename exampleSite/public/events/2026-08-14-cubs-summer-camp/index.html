Cubs Summer Camp

14 August 2026, 00:00 · Pioneer Camp, Buckinghamshire

Three days under canvas at Pioneer Camp — Friday afternoon arrival, Sunday afternoon collection. The Pack will be running a mix of campcraft, water activities and the all-time-favourite night hike.

Kit list and consent forms will be sent home four weeks ahead of camp. Bursary support is available — speak to Akela in confidence if cost would otherwise stop your Cub joining us.
