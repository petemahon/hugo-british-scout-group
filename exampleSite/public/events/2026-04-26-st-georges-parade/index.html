St George's Day Parade

26 April 2026, 09:30 · Anytown Parish Church

The whole Group joins our District for the annual St George’s Day Parade. Meet at Anytown Parish Church for inspection at 09:30, then we march to the war memorial for the act of remembrance and the Scout promise.

Parents are very welcome to come along and watch.
