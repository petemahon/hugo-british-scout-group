Mobile phones

Phones aren't needed at camp, and we'd encourage you to leave yours at home. If you do bring one, please hand it to a leader when you arrive — we'll keep it safe and give it back at the end. Leaders carry a Group phone for emergencies, and you'll have a contact number for the whole camp, so there's always a way to reach us.

Travelling from abroad

If your camp is across a border from where you live, also pack: passport plus a photocopy kept separately; your GHIC/EHIC card and travel-insurance details; a little local currency for tuck; a travel adapter; and a local-pharmacy equivalent of any UK-only brand you rely on (sun cream, antihistamines), as familiar brands may not be sold locally.

Please leave at home

Please leave at home: phones and tablets (see below), valuables, sweets and fizzy drinks. We provide all food and water.
