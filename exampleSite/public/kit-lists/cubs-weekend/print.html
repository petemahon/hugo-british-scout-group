Mobile phones

Phones aren't needed at camp, and we'd encourage you to leave yours at home. If you do bring one, please hand it to a leader when you arrive — we'll keep it safe and give it back at the end. Leaders carry a Group phone for emergencies, and you'll have a contact number for the whole camp, so there's always a way to reach us.

Please leave at home

Please leave at home: phones, tablets and games consoles (see the note above), money beyond a few pounds for the tuck shop, and anything valuable or irreplaceable. Sweets and fizzy drinks stay at home too — we provide all the food.
