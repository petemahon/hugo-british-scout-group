Welcome to 1st Anytown Scouts

1st Anytown Scouts provides fun and friendship, challenge and everyday adventure to over 300 young people in Anytown, for both boys and girls aged 6 to 18.

Joining us couldn't be easier

Register your interest to join 1st Anytown Scouts. We welcome everyone to join us, and will give priority to young people who have a parent willing to volunteer some of their time to help out. No previous experience necessary!

If you would like to both volunteer and register a Young Person, please ensure you complete both entry forms on this page.

Join 1st Anytown Scouts

For each young person you would like to register, fill out an entry form below. Adults above the age of 18 are welcome to volunteer and do not need to have a child in Scouting.

Who can join

BSO is a slice of UK Scouting, here for families living in Anytown. Whether you've moved here from the UK or somewhere else, our doors are open.

Every country has its own home of Scouting. Here, that's Anytown Scout Association — and if you're a national of Anytown, they'd love to have you.

Volunteering's a different story. Anyone over 18 is welcome to help out, whatever your nationality — including nationals of Anytown.

(This follows POR 3.2.1.1 — The Scout Association's rule for every BSO group.)
