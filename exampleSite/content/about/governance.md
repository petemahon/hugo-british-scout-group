Every parent and carer of a member is welcome at the AGM, where the
Trustee Board reports on the year, presents the accounts, and stands
for re-election. We'd genuinely welcome more hands on the board -
particularly anyone with finance, fundraising or premises experience.
If that might be you, have a word with any of the trustees listed
above.
