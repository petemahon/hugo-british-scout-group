Six teams went head-to-head at this year's Cooking Cup, and frankly
the standard was alarming. We had perfectly-timed pasta, a passable
attempt at hollandaise, and one team who tried to make profiteroles on
a Trangia (we admire the ambition).

The judges (your faithful Pack Leaders, plus an unsuspecting parent
who'd come to collect) deliberated for forty minutes before declaring
the Yellow Six victors by a single point. Their menu: tomato soup,
chicken fajitas, and a tray of brownies that disappeared before the
photograph was taken.

Recipes will be added to the Pack's recipe book at next week's
meeting. Bring your own copy.
