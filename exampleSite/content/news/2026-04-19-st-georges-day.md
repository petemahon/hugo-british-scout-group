Sunday morning kicked off with a downpour and a flurry of texts asking
whether the parade was still on. By 10am the sun was out, the church
hall was full of polished shoes, and the piper had arrived right on
schedule.

Beavers led the way (as is tradition for our youngest), with Cubs
behind, Scouts holding the colours, and Explorers bringing up the
rear. The new neckers issued at investiture last week made their first
formal outing, which was very smart indeed.

Our thanks to the parents who turned out in numbers, to the church for
hosting us, and to the piper - who has politely declined our requests
for an encore at every weekly meeting since.

Promise renewals next week.
