## How the year works

We run three terms a year, broadly mapping to the local school
calendar. Each section publishes a [termly programme](/programme/)
in the first week - a week-by-week list of what's happening, plus the
Skills for Life themes we're covering.

## The fixed points

A handful of dates every family should know about:

- **St George's Day Parade** - late April. The whole Group parades
  through the town in uniform.
- **Cub Camp / Scout Camp / Explorer Expedition** - every summer term.
  Dates vary by section.
- **Remembrance Sunday** - November. Uniform attendance at the local
  war memorial.
- **Group Christmas Party** - last meeting of the autumn term.
- **AGM** - March. Open to all families.

## Where to see what's coming up

The [Events page](/events/) carries the full diary with downloadable
calendar feeds (`.ics`). Subscribe once and every camp, parade and
training session lands in your calendar app.
