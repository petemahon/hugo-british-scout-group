## The waiting list

Add your child via the [Join us page](/join/) — there's a card per
section showing whether we have spaces or a waiting list. Each card has
a link to either join the waiting list directly (we use Online Scout
Manager — OSM) or to email the section leader.

When a place comes up the leader will be in touch with trial-week
dates. After two or three trial weeks, we'll invite you to enrol
formally.

## Subs

Subs vary by section — see the cards on the [Join us page](/join/)
for the current term's amount. We don't charge a joining fee; subs
cover the term's running costs, equipment hire, weekly badges and
contribute to camp subsidies.

If subs would be a barrier for your family, please tell us — we have a
quiet hardship policy and no child has ever been turned away over
money.

## Uniform

A necker (£5) is the only thing you need on day one. The blue uniform
shirt and badges come once your child is formally enrolled. We can
direct you to local stockists.

## First-night reminders

Comfortable clothes you don't mind getting dirty. Trainers, not school
shoes. Water bottle. Layers — we go outside when we can.

## Camps — what to pack

Once your child is settling in, camps are where Scouting really comes
alive. We publish a [kit list for every kind of camp](/kit-lists/) —
weekends, summer camp, expeditions — so you know exactly what to pack
(and what to leave at home). Each camp's event page links straight to
the right list, and you can print any of them as a tick-off sheet.
