This page describes how our Group applies The Scout Association's
safeguarding rules. It links to the authoritative policy rather than
reproducing it.

## We follow Scouts UK safeguarding

1st Anytown Scouts is a member group of The Scout Association. Every
leader is DBS-checked (or holds the host-country equivalent), trained,
and operates under the Association's safeguarding policy and the "Yellow
Card" safe-from-harm rules at every meeting and camp.

- Safeguarding policy and the Yellow Card:
  <https://www.scouts.org.uk/safeguarding/>
- Policy, Organisation and Rules (POR): <https://www.scouts.org.uk/por/>

## Reporting a concern

If you are worried about a young person's safety, contact our Group Lead
Volunteer straight away. If a child is in immediate danger, call the
emergency services.
