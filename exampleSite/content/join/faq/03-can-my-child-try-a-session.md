Yes — every section runs a few trial weeks at the start of each term.
Once you're on the waiting list, the section leader will be in touch
with try-it dates as places come up.

We don't run open evenings as a rule — section meetings are busy and
better suited to one child at a time joining in for a few weeks before
formally enrolling.
