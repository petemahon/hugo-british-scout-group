Meetings run from arrival through investiture-style opening to a mix of
games, badge work and outdoor activity, then a closing circle. Squirrels
and Beavers run shorter sessions; Cubs, Scouts and Explorers run longer.
Specific timings per section are on the cards above.

Most weeks we're indoors at the hut, but we head outside whenever the
weather and programme allow. Patrol/Six leaders run smaller groups
within the meeting.
